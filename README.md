# Predictive Modeling of Coronary Heart Disease 💓
I developed a heart disease prediction pipeline where I performed model selection across three architectures. I prioritized Logistic Regression due to its superior performance in minimizing False Negatives and its statistical interpretability (p&lt;0.01).
